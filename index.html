Welcome to Save-A-Shot

Our Mission

While creating Save-A-Shot, we aimed for the interests on the individual in need. With the Coronavirus 2019 still running rampant, we wanted to provide the ability to search up information of the virus, dependent on the location you are in. Save-A-Shot was created so that you and your family may better be aware of the what is taking place in the pandemic, all around you.

What We Provide

Our services are that of many:

  • Information on how widespread the virus is in your area
  • Map locations of where clinics/hospitals that provide COVID 19 services may be found near you
  • Ability to seek out links of where a vaccination can be scheduled (CA residents only)
...and much more!

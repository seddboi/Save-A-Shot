Our Mission

 As the pandemic continues to spread wildly around us, we have all had to face the stress and uncertainty of how our safety and health could result in the end. So with Save-A-Shot, we wanted to create a universal mapping program that could assist in keeping you safe from the Coronavirus 2019. With the vaccine still being relatively new, there is much uncertainty and confusion of how and where you could get the vaccine. Whether it be for yourself or your loved ones, Save-A-Shot was made out to create a universal guide to locating and scheduling vaccination appointments, at the simple click of a button.

How We Can Help

If you reside within California:

  • We provide information on how widespread the virus is in your area
  • We present a localized map of nearby clinics/hospitals/pharmacies that provide COVID 19 vaccination services
  • We also provide the ability to follow another link to schedule a vaccination appointment (where availability and accessibility allows)

If you reside outside of California:

 ** At this moment, our services only allow for coverage of residents that live within California. This is due to the very early stages of vaccination tracking systems that are open for use. We hope to soon provide coverage for the remainder of states within the United States and its territories **
